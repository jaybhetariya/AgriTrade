Grains Listings

Peanut

Peanuts, also known as groundnuts, are a staple crop in many regions. They are rich in protein and healthy fats, making them a valuable commodity. Our peanuts are sourced directly from local farmers, ensuring freshness and quality.

Wheat

Wheat is a fundamental grain used in the production of bread, pasta, and other food products. We procure wheat from reliable sources, ensuring its quality and suitability for various applications.

Jeera

Jeera, or cumin, is a spice widely used in culinary applications. It is known for its distinct aroma and flavor. We offer high- quality jeera sourced from trusted growers, ensuring its purity and potency.

Moong

Moong, or green gram, is a versatile legume used in various dishes. It is a good source of protein and fiber. Our moong is carefully selected to meet our quality standards, providing a nutritious option for consumers.

Chana

Chana, or chickpeas, are a popular legume used in various dishes. They are a good source of protein and fiber. We offer high- quality chana sourced from trusted farmers, ensuring its nutritional value and taste.

Tal

Tal, or sesame seeds, are valued for their oil content and nutty flavor. They are used in both culinary and industrial applications. Our tal is sourced from reputable growers, ensuring its quality and purity.
